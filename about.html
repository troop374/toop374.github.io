About the Troop


Troop 374 is a moderate-sized troop with a dynamic membership which engages in a variety of activites chosen by the scouts.

Scouting is a year-round Boy Scouts of America program designed for boys who are between the ages of 11 and 18 years old.

Parents, leaders, and organizations work together to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law.
