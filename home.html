Scoutmaster's Minute

Welcome to Troop 374, an active Troop within the North Star District of the Occoneechee Council! We serve the youth of Raleigh and surrounding areas and are chartered by Saint Andrews Presbyterian Church. We have patrols for boys starting at 6th grade. We have an incredible volunteer staff dedicated to serving and preparing our youth to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law. We believe that every child deserves a well-trained leader, and our leaders have extensive training to ensure that our youth have the best experience possible.
